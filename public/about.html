About ScrinDeck and how it works

ScrinDeck turns a browser into a signage player and another browser into the remote. Use it to compose layouts, run media, overlay a news-style ticker, and keep repeat playback available.

How to use ScrinDeck.

The system has two parts: the display player that stays on the screen, and the control panel where the operator uploads, arranges, schedules, and updates content.

1

Open the display player

On the TV, kiosk, tablet, or media device, open /display.html and switch the browser to full screen.

2

Sign in to the remote

Open /control.html on your phone, laptop, or tablet and sign in with the admin password.

3

Upload and assign media

Add images and videos, build the active playlist, then choose what appears in each layout region.

4

Publish live changes

Adjust layouts, ticker text, schedules, colors, and backgrounds while connected displays update in real time.

Operating notes.

Keep the first playlist load online so the display device can cache media. For production, use persistent hosting storage and a strong admin password.

A

Storage matters

Uploaded media and saved settings should live on durable storage so deploys do not erase them.

B

Cache needs a first load

If a device is new or its browser cache was cleared, open the display online once before relying on offline play.
