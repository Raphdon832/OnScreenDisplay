OnScreen Signage

Manage media playlists, text slides, and scheduled notices for digital displays.

Open Display Player Sign in to Control Panel

How to Use:

  1. Open the Display Player on your screen or media device (full screen with F11)
  2. Open the Control Panel on your phone, tablet, or computer
  3. Use the control panel to:
    • Upload image and video assets
    • Build a repeatable media playlist
    • Show text slides or scheduled notices
  4. Use arrow keys ← → to navigate, Esc to clear
